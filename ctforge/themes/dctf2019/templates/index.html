Deep Space DAIS CTF (DCTF) is a Capture The Flag hacking contest in which a number of alien races fight independently against each other in order to tower over the galactic empire.

The goal of each team is to maintain a set of services such that they remain available and uncompromised throughout the contest. Each team also has to attempt to compromise the other teams' services. Since all the teams have access to a copy of the same virtual machine containing the vulnerable services, each team has to find vulnerabilities in its host and fix them without disrupting the services.

At the same time, the teams have to leverage their knowledge about the vulnerabilities they find to compromise the servers run by other teams. Compromising a service allows a team to bypass the service's security mechanisms and to "capture the flag" associated with the service. The team that has the best combination of attack and defense skills wins.

In parallel with the main contest, a number of side challenges are left to be solved to the bravest participants. Since points earned by solving challenges do not contribute to the score of the teams, these problems must be solved individually.
